Simpe Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs

cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats.



Prepation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Prepation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes


Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or all
  • Optional fillings:Cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablrdpoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tlit the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings(optional):Whene the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle.spinkle your chosen fillings over half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:Ax the omellette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and foid it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute.then slide it anto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot,with additional salt and pepperif needed.
