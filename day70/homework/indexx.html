POWER DOES MATTER



At the heart of every Mustang is a high-powered engine. Mustang offers four available engines,
including the 5.2L supercharged V8.









ExhoBoost®



It’s a first Ecoboost® engine powered by Ford Performance
and it’s not afraid to prove it. The 2.3L high-performance
engine differentiates itself with a slightly larger twin-scroll
turbo compressor.




  • 310 horsepower

  • 350 lb.-ft. of torque*

  • Turbocharging and direct injection

  • Twin independent variable camshaft timing (Ti-VCT)

  • Twin independent variable camshaft timing (Ti-VCT)




GT



5.0L Ti-VCT V8
An engine that comes standard with a hit of adrenaline.




  • 460 horsepower

  • 420 lb.-ft. of tire-squealing torque*

  • Port fuel direct injection




Mach 1



5.0L Tri-VCT V8
The pinnacle of 5-liter performance, this engine is uniquely
tuned andcalibrated for speed, And with the Mach 1
Handling Package.



  • 480 horsepower at 7,000 rpm

  • 420 lb.-ft. of torque* at 4,600rpm

  • Two side heat exchanges for track endurance

  • 6-speed manual transmission with rev-matching


Shelby® GT500®



5.2L supercharged cross=plane crank V8
Top-of-the-line power that you can feel.



  • 760 horsepower

  • 625 lb.-ft. of torque*


Standard on the Shelby GT500 x
